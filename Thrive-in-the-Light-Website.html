How Lifewave Phototherapy Works

Lifewave patches use a process called photobiomodulation — the use of light to influence biological processes in the body. It's been studied for decades and forms the basis of Lifewave's entire product range.

The Simple Explanation

Your body naturally produces heat, which emits infrared light. Lifewave patches contain a layer of organic crystals that are designed to capture that infrared energy and reflect specific wavelengths back into your skin.

This reflected light signal is designed to trigger certain responses in your body — responses your body is naturally capable of — without adding any substance to your system. No drugs. No supplements. Nothing enters your bloodstream.

The X39® Mechanism: GHK-Cu Peptide Elevation

Lifewave's flagship product, X39®, works by elevating a naturally occurring peptide in the body called GHK-Cu. This compound tends to decline with age, and research suggests it plays a role in cellular repair, energy regulation, and vitality.

X39® uses phototherapy to encourage your body's own production of GHK-Cu — using light, not chemistry. Clinical research on this mechanism forms part of Lifewave's published science portfolio.

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions I hear most often.

What are Lifewave patches?

Lifewave patches are wearable phototherapy devices. They sit on the skin and use your body's own infrared energy — reflected back through organic crystals in the patch — to support certain biological processes. Nothing enters your body. They are not drugs, supplements, or medical devices.

Do anything from the patches enter my body?

No. Lifewave patches are non-transdermal — meaning nothing crosses the skin barrier. They work entirely through light interaction on the skin's surface. This is one of the things that makes them genuinely different from most wellness products.

Are they safe?

Lifewave patches are drug-free and non-invasive. Because nothing enters your body, they have a very different safety profile from medications or supplements. A small number of people experience mild skin sensitivity from the adhesive, which is common with any patch-style product. As with any wellness product, it's worth mentioning to your GP if you have specific health conditions or concerns.

Do they actually work?

Results vary from person to person — as with any wellness approach. Many people notice something within the first few days to few weeks of consistent use. Some people take longer. A small number don't notice a significant effect. The best way to know if they work for you is to try them — and the 90-day journey tends to give the fullest picture.

Which patch should I start with?

Most people start with X39® — it's Lifewave's flagship product and supports general whole-body wellness, energy, sleep, and recovery. If everyday physical comfort is your main concern, IceWave™ is often a better starting point. If you're not sure, feel free to ask me directly and I'll point you in the right direction.

Can I wear them with other patches?

Yes — many Lifewave products are designed to be used in combination. X39® and Energy Enhancer, for example, are often worn together. Lifewave provides placement guides for each combination. I'm happy to share more detail on this if you'd like to know more.

How long do I wear each patch?

Most patches are designed to be worn for up to 12 hours, then removed and disposed of. They're single-use, so a fresh patch each day. Some people wear them during the day; others prefer overnight (particularly for sleep support).
